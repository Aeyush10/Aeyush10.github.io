About

I'm a Research Fellow at the PROSE team at Microsoft, where I work on agentic AI tools for software and spreadsheet development, guided by Dr. Emerson Murphy-Hill. By studying interactions with such tools, I aim to understand how we can improve them to make simple and effective tools for users. I recently completed my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sruti Ragavan. During this time, I investigated the opportunities and challenges of using LLMs for programming education.

My research interests lie at the intersection of human-AI interactions (HAI), software engineering (SE), and programming education. I'm excited by the opportunities created by the rise in the accessibility and proficiency of generative AI in democratizing software development and education, and firmly believe that understanding how and why people use these tools is key to realizing their full potential.
