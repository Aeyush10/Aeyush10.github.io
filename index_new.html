About Me

I am a Human-Computer Interaction researcher with a focus on understanding and improving the relationship between humans and technology. My work explores the intersection of user experience, artificial intelligence, and interactive systems.

Currently, I am investigating trust in generative AI systems among students, exploring how these technologies impact learning and education. My research combines quantitative and qualitative methods to understand user perceptions and behaviors in human-AI interaction.
